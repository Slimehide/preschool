Frequently Asked Questions,
Answered.

What is the minimum contract length?

There are no minimum contract terms - you can cancel at any time and for any reason. For example, if your enrollment numbers are high and you no longer need our Enrollment Boost plan, you can cancel our services without any notice required.

What does unlimited mean? Is it actually unlimited design and development work?

You can submit an unlimited number of website design and website development requests. Our team will work on your requests one at a time through a queue. Simple requests, such as updating text or links will be done within one business day. More complex requests, such as redesigning a website page, may take multiple days to complete.

Does the $250 per month include advertising spend?

No, the $250 per month covers our management of your preschool's Google and Facebook ad accounts and does not include the budget for ad spend. We suggest $300 per month or more in ad spend for the best results.

How quickly can I expect to increase my enrollment?

If you have a professionally designed website optimized for converting website visitors into parent walkthroughs and are subscribed to our Enrollment Boost plan, you can expect to get 1+ enrollment for every $300 in ad spend.

On average, our clients receive an interested parent inquiry through our managed ads at a cost of $30 per interested parent inquiry. That means that for $300, you can expect around 10 interested parent inquiries. If you can convert just 10% of interested parent inquiries into an enrollment, you can expect 1+ enrollment for every $300 in ad spend.
